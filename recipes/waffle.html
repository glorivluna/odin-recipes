Waffles

Description

Chaffles are an overnight sensation in the low carb/keto world. Adding almond flour gives them a more traditional waffle texture and helps get rid of any eggy taste. Seriously, try them, they're delicious! I use an inexpensive mini waffle maker (such as DashÂ® Mini Maker Waffle) which produces perfect 4-inch chaffles. So many ways to use chaffles, the sky's the limit!

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon blanched almond flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • cooking spray
